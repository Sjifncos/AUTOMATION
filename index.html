What is Automation in the Industrial Revolution?

Definition

Automation is the technology by which a process or procedure is performed with minimal human assistance.
It increases efficiency, accuracy, and productivity.

Historical Context

Since the beginning, everyday people and business owners have always looked for different ways to improve efficiency in day-to-day tasks.

Evolution

In ancient times, our ancestors did most of their work manually with the assistance of simple machines, pulleys, levers, hand tools, and sometimes working animals.
